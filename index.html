About

Hi, I’m Afdil Saputra. When I was 10, I became fascinated with my uncle’s computer, spending hours exploring and learning how things worked. By the time I was 12, my curiosity grew so strong that I convinced my father to buy me a laptop. That’s when my interest in computers truly began to take shape.

After graduating high school, I pursued a degree in Informatics Engineering, where I was introduced to coding and web design. From that point on, I dedicated myself to developing my skills, continuously improving and challenging myself to grow as a web developer and designer.

One developer I truly look up to is Bryan Braun, whose work and approach to coding inspire me to continuously improve my craft.
